OPEN DAY

Every year India celebrates ‘National science day’ on February 28. National science day is celebrated to pay the tribute to the discovery of the ‘Raman Effect’ by Sir C.V. Raman
IIITDM KURNOOL took an initiative in this regard and commenced the first ‘Open day’ science event on occasion of NATIONAL SCIENCE DAY on 28th February 2020 with the theme of “Women in Science” as decided by the Department of Science and technology under the Head of the Department, Dr. D Murali.
The open day was immensely filled with the glory of science all the day. It included various workshops, quizzes, experiments, lab tours, arts, sky gazing with telescope, seminar , music , invaluable and interactive lectures by the following great scientists of India.


SPEAKER'S PROFILE :

:


The First speaker at Open day was Dr. Ramakrishna Bhamidipati, Scientist and Group director at Armour Technology Centre. He is working in Defence Research and Development Organisation(the famous DRDO), Government of India. He also worked for Agni Missile technology. He has 20 international publications and two international patents.

The Second speaker was Dr. N. Lakshmi Narayana, scientist , engineer and the Deputy General Manager (DGM) for the control Centre Facilities Group(CCFG) of Communication and Control Centre Area(CCCA) of ISRO Telemetry Tracking and Command Network(ISTRAC), Banglore. He worked in various programs such as support for IRS-1C satellite, IRS-P6 Satellite, Chandrayan-1, Mars Orbital Mission (MOM).

The Third speaker was Dr. Jayakumar Venketesan, Chief Technology Officer for Synergy Moon (One of the five finalists in the Google Lunar X prize challenge). He is an Astropreneur and CEO for “Valles Marineris International Private Limited”, India. He is also a Nano-Satellite Researcher and Chief Technology officer for KSF Space Foundation, UK.
