WEBINAR - 4

THE NATURE OF DISCOVERY IN PHYSICS

As a part of exciting Monday popular talks in IIITDM Kurnool, An eloquent lecture was given by Prof.S.V.M. Satyanarayana .The talk was about how the art of thinking and development in the discoveries of physics evolved. He brieflyexplained the whole history of scientists from Plato to Einstein, their thoughts and discoveries.
SPEAKER : Prof .S.V.M. Satyanarayana
TOPIC : THE NATURE OF DISCOVERY IN PHYSICS
DATE : : 28-10-2019 28-10-2019
SPEAKER'S PROFILE : Prof .S.V.M. Satyanarayana is a Professor at the Department of Physics at Pondicherry University. His area of expertise is Nonlinear Dynamics, Statistical Mechanics, Biophysics and computational physics.
